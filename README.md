## Background

Since 1999, I've had the privilege of shipping exceptional products to customers around the world. I've led teams ranging in size from 1 to 100 at start-ups and Fortune 500 companies.

I graduated from business school in 2002 with a degree in Management Information Systems. In my junior year of college, I was hired by LexisNexis where I eventually led small and large teams shipping a wide range of large-scale
enterprise products.

In the recent past, I owned and operated a distinguished consulting firm — Littlelines — specializing in shipping products for small and large companies worldwide. For fourteen years, I led engineering and design teams shipping
hundreds of products for our clients. In 2020, the company was acquired.

Today, I'm the CEO of StaticPop Co., a small portfolio of digital companies I own or have invested in. I'm currently available for investment opportunities, business coaching, product strategy, speaking, and mentoring. If interested, please feel free to reach out.
